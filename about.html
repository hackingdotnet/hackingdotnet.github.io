Why .Net?

Why not? There are more programming languages than one realizes. As a computer scientist it's important to understand the basic constructs of languages in general. This is why many undergraduate programs include a survey course around programming languages. This trend carries over to careers in software engineering and development. This is easily seen in Web Development where having knowledge in static and dynamic languages is almost a necessity.

This site will be dedicated to helping developers level up on C#, the .Net Framework, and TypeScript. The kata's that will be developed will be geared toward understanding the fundamental concepts of computer science. These topics will begin with basic object oriented principles such as inheritance, encapsulation, and polymorphism. It will continue through data structures and algorithm analysis. There will also be course work for operating systems and database systems.
